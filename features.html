Features

Built for Performance.

A complete toolkit bringing professional training structure to your daily routine — for gym, running, cycling, and beyond.

Structure

Training Clarity

Stop guessing what to do next. TrainQ builds professional-grade plans tailored to your specific goals and adapts them as you progress.

  • ✓ Goal-Oriented Plans: 5k, 10k, Marathon, Cycling, Gym, or General Fitness.
  • ✓ Smart Scheduling: Workouts placed optimally based on your availability.
  • ✓ Session Details: Exact pace, heart rate, and duration for every interval.
Adaptive Engine

Adjusts to Reality

Miss a workout? Sick? Tired? TrainQ automatically recalibrates your plan so you never feel "behind".

  • ✓ Auto-Rescheduling: Shifts missed sessions instantly, no manual effort.
  • ✓ Fatigue Detection: Recognizes when you need a rest day before you do.
  • ✓ Progressive Overload: Increases intensity only when you're truly ready.
Analytics

True Progress Insight

Go beyond raw data. Understand if you are actually improving with clean, actionable metrics that matter.

  • ✓ Fitness Trends: Visualize fitness vs. fatigue over time.
  • ✓ Race Prediction: Estimate your times based on recent training data.
  • ✓ Health Sync: Integrates with Apple Health & Garmin Connect.
