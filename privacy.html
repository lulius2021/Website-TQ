4. Processed Data Categories

4.1 Website

Log data (IP address, user agent) to ensure stability and security.

4.2 App Data

Account info (email, ID) and your inputs (training plans, workouts).

4.3 Garmin Data (only with consent)

If you connect your Garmin account, we process data only as required for features:

  • Activity summaries (duration, distance, pace, HR)
  • Activity details (laps, samples) if needed for analysis
  • We do NOT store your Garmin password.

5. Purpose and Legal Basis

We process data to provide the TrainQ service (Art. 6.1.b GDPR), to ensure security (Art. 6.1.f GDPR), and based on your consent for health/Garmin data (Art. 6.1.a & Art. 9.2.a GDPR).

6. Garmin Integration Details

  • Data Minimization: We only fetch what is needed.
  • No Sale: We do not sell your data.
  • Control: You can disconnect and revoke permissions at any time.
  • See Garmin's Privacy Policy: https://www.garmin.com/privacy/connect
